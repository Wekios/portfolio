Scrap Jump is a endless runner game where the player plays the role of a tire avoiding obstacles by slowing it down. On this project a team of 5 members worked (two programmers, two designers and music composer) . The game is located on a junkyard and obstacles are made up of two parts. The lower part of the old cars that are moving on a conveyor belt and the upper part of the magnet that moves along the rail at the same speed as the lower part of the barrier.

ScoreApp is an application that is designed to monitor the result of card games (for now Remi, Mau-Mau, the plan is to add other games) . When user opens the app it shows him a selection of games. After selecting the game, set up certain parameters such as how many points until victory in-game, and names of the players. When you fill out all the information screen is depicting the players in the form of cards.
